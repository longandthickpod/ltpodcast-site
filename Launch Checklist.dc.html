How the newsletter capture works

Right now every signup is saved in the visitor's browser, and you can export them all to a spreadsheet anytime via the "· owner ·" link at the bottom of the Newsletter page. To collect them in one Google Sheet automatically, do this once:

  1. Make a Google Sheet with columns: ts, name, email, goal, source.
  2. In the Sheet: Extensions → Apps Script. Paste a short doPost script (ask me for the exact text) and Deploy → Web app → access: Anyone.
  3. Copy the web-app URL it gives you.
  4. Paste it into the Newsletter page's "Google Sheet / CRM endpoint URL" setting. Done — new signups now land in your Sheet automatically.

When the list grows, you'll likely move to a dedicated newsletter tool (Beehiiv, ConvertKit, Mailchimp) that both stores subscribers and sends the email. Ask me and I'll wire the form straight to one of those instead.
