The hosts

About

Two regular guys from the music and skating worlds who got obsessed with health and somewhere along the way fell hard for helping people feel better in their own bodies.

Ronnie

Grew up in skateparks dodging the law — until someone fixed his injuries like actual sorcery. Now he's obsessed with movement and corrective exercise, and how the body really works.

Daniel

Grew up in the music scene as the runt of the room, then spent years trying everything to build strength and health — making all the mistakes so the listeners don't have to.
